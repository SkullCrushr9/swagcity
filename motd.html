KOS Rules

  • You cannot KOS/KILL anyone who warned for blocking/following even if the victim is inno
  • Self implicated KOS can be followed
  • Self defense is a valid reason to KOS/Kill someone
  • If you have DNA off an Inno/Det body, KOS (ensure DNA icon is leading to correct person)
  • You must KOS a player if they have done a T act
  • "Player A is a T" is a valid KOS
  • Using the TTT Radio T bind is a valid KOS
  • "Kill Player A" is a valid KOS
  • You must have evidence to back up a KOS, especially in voice chat, if not the KOS will be considered false
  • If a player calls a KOS that leads to an inno death, it can be considered a T act if they did not provide a reason. If a reason is provided, you may not kill the player until overtime. They're considered "High Sus"
  • KOZ, KO5 are not valid KOS's, however if used to trick players, the slays will be added onto you, and if determined to be group trolls, slays will be added to both the attacker and sayer.
  • You must give 3 warnings (3 seconds in between) to people shooting in the air, following, grappling, or blocking you before you may KOS them

Traitorous Acts

  • Witnessing/damaging an Innocent/Detective with a prop or gun, throwing a discombob in a dangerous area/causes dmg or kills
  • Attempted Propkill (swinging a prop around vigorously at someone, or slinging a prop full-force in someone's direction)
  • Not identifying bodies (exempt: if there is a pile of bodies, and all but 1 are unid'd they pass over it, you can't kill them, if player is engaged in active firefight, you may not kill them if UNID'd is next to them)(ask to ID bodies, neglect to ID leads to KOS)
  • Killing a player w/o cause (KOS players are exempt)
  • Seeing two players enter a room, hear gunfire and death, and the other player leaves the room with an UNID
  • Damaging Testers/Health Stations
  • Tested as a Traitor
  • Holding Traitor weapons out (unless proven by D)
  • Planting/Arming C4, Tripwires, not damaged by turret/not triggering melon mine, using any T items
  • Hiding UNID'd bodies
  • Using an explosive prop near players/throwing an incen
  • Wielding T props in specific maps
  • Entering or leaving a T room (MC CITY DARK IS EXEMPT)
  • Opening a T trap (must be in a secluded area where another T couldn't have opened said trap)
  • Last words that KOS a player on a body
  • Shooting at or near players/flinging props near or at people (traitor baiting)
  • If you have DNA off an Inno/Det body, KOS (ensure DNA icon is leading to correct person)
  • Blocking a player in an area of danger (IE C4, Incen, Holy Hand, Banana Bomb, etc)

High Sus Acts

Are not KOSable until Overtime

  • A player in a room that has a t room door and was not seen entering or in the room previously
  • Hearing a T room door open nearby and seeing a player near the area (must be a room)
  • Hearing a gun fight in a place nearby and a player running away from the carnage. *Can be a KOS if they run over the UNID'd body without IDing it
  • Hearing a T weapon in use by a player nearby, but you do not witness them holding the weapon. If there is more than 1 you may high sus other players involved
  • Passing a C4, Tripwire
  • Around a KOS'd player
  • Refusing to test (Must be commanded by a detective)
  • Player A passes near Player B at the end of a long corridor with no exits who is running in the other direction. A few seconds later, Player A sees an unidentified body in the middle of the corridor where Player B came from. Since there are no other exits and no gunshots were heard until reaching the body (hinting someone was killed), it proves Player B have passed near this body without IDing it. Therefore, Player A may place high sus on Player B
  • Self defense [not kosable] [Player B shoots you, you kill Player B, Player C watched it all, Player B ended up being innocent, Player C may place a High Suspicion on you]
  • You see two players in a room, hear gunfire and a deathscream and walk in to find a recently ID'd as inno/detective, you may place high sus on the player.
  • Live checks only lead to a high sus if player does not respond
  • Defending a KOS'd traitor, or lying in the behalf of a KOS'd traitor

Guilty By Association/GBA List

  • Player A shoots Player C, and Player B witnesses it does nothing about it - Player B is GBA
  • Player A kills Player C and Player B does not ID the body - Player B is GBA
  • Player A enters a Traitor room and Player B witnesses that, but does not call out Player A - Player B is GBA
  • Player A plants a C4/T wep and runs. Player B sees that but does not call out Player A - Player B is GBA
  • Player A and Player B are having a firefight, Player C walks in and watched the battle, the Player who wins the battle and either IDs or leaves the body UNID'd is GBA. However if the body is ID'd as a T, the surviving player is not GBA, and Player C is not GBA
  • Player A kills Player B, and Player B is ID'd inno, or not ID'd and Player C watched the entire fight and did nothing - Player A and C are GBA
  • AFK bodies around UNID'd bodies and is the only one(s) standing and the UNID'ds are ID'd as innos - the AFK(s) are GBA
